OUR MUSIC CLASSES

Our school is a solution for families who would like to expose their children to the world of music

Our classes help to develop the skills necessary for music learning and a lifelong enjoyment of music.

We have programs for everyone. In addition to teaching music to children, we instruct adults and seniors

Private music lessons provide one-on-one attention, so teachers can focus on an individual student’s needs.

1.Instrumental Classes:

Piano: Learn to play the piano with our experienced instructors who provide personalized lessons focusing on technique, music theory, and repertoire.

Guitar: Master the guitar with our comprehensive lessons covering chords, scales, strumming patterns, and music notation.

Drums: Get your rhythm on with our drum lessons, where you'll learn drumming techniques, coordination, and how to play along with your favorite songs.

Violin: Learn to play the piano with our experienced instructors who provide personalized lessons focusing on technique, music theory, and repertoire.

Guitar: Master the guitar with our comprehensive lessons covering chords, scales, strumming patterns, and music notation.

Harmoniam: Master the guitar with our comprehensive lessons covering chords, scales, strumming patterns, and music notation.

2.Vocal Classes:

Singing: Discover your voice and develop your vocal range, tone, and performance skills in our singing classes led by experienced vocal coaches.

Music Theory: Understand the building blocks of music through our theory classes, covering topics such as scales, chords, rhythm, harmony, and ear training. Composition: Unleash your creativity and learn the art of composing music, whether it's for solo instruments, ensembles, or even film scoring.

3.Specialty Classes:

Music Production: Explore the world of music production using software like FL studio, and learn to create your own tracks and beats.

Workshops: Participate in workshops covering various topics such as performance techniques, music history, and career development in the music industry.

Private Classes:

One-on-One Instruction: Benefit from personalized attention and tailored lesson plans with our private one-on-one lessons, available for all instruments and vocal training.
