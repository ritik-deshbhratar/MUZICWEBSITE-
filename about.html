About

AKS is an online live music learning platform for all age groups. You can learn Guitar, Keyboard, Piano, Hindustani Music , All form of light music (Bollywood(Old New) , Ghazal ,Bhajan Vocal and instrument.

The classes are conducted live one to one on zoom or Google meet video platform. We also coach students for Music Grade Exams for Guitar, keyboard, Piano, conducted by Trinity College of Music, London & Rockschool, London.

The music classes are one to one, which gives space for tutor and student to engage and students have significant learning outcomes in a short duration. Whether you are taking your first steps in your musical journey or looking for advanced skills and music grade certifications, with aks be assured you will not only acquire skills to improve playing ability, but expand your technical proficiency with advanced courses and music theory, all this while learning to play and sing your most favorite songs.

We are here for provide

Welcome to our music class! We offer a diverse range of music classes tailored to suit students of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're a beginner looking to explore the fundamentals or an experienced musician aiming to refine your technique, we have something for everyone. Here are the details of our classes:

1.Instrumental Classes:

Piano: Learn to play the piano with our experienced instructors who provide personalized lessons focusing on technique, music theory, and repertoire.

Guitar: Master the guitar with our comprehensive lessons covering chords, scales, strumming patterns, and music notation.

Drums: Get your rhythm on with our drum lessons, where you'll learn drumming techniques, coordination, and how to play along with your favorite songs.

2.Vocal Classes:

Singing: Discover your voice and develop your vocal range, tone, and performance skills in our singing classes led by experienced vocal coaches.

Music Theory: Understand the building blocks of music through our theory classes, covering topics such as scales, chords, rhythm, harmony, and ear training. Composition: Unleash your creativity and learn the art of composing music, whether it's for solo instruments, ensembles, or even film scoring.

3.Specialty Classes:

Music Production: Explore the world of music production using software like FL studio, and learn to create your own tracks and beats.

Workshops: Participate in workshops covering various topics such as performance techniques, music history, and career development in the music industry.

Private Classes:

One-on-One Instruction: Benefit from personalized attention and tailored lesson plans with our private one-on-one lessons, available for all instruments and vocal training.

At our music class, we are dedicated to fostering a nurturing and inspiring learning environment where students can explore their passion for music, unleash their creativity, and achieve their musical goals. Join us on this musical journey today!
